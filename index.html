RECIPE: "THE PROGRAMMER'S PIZZA"

Ingredients:


1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)


1 cup pizza sauce


2 cups Mozzarella cheese, shredded


1/2 red bell pepper, sliced


1/2 green bell pepper, sliced


1/2 onion, sliced


1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced


1/2 cup olives, sliced


1/2 cup spinach, optional


1/2 tsp Oregano


1/2 tsp crushed red pepper


1/2 tsp garlic powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to its highest temperature, usually around 500 degrees Fahrenheit or 260 degrees Celsius.
  2. While the oven is heating, prepare your toppings.
  3. You'll want to slice your vegetables thin so they cook properly.
  4. Stretch out your pizza dough on a floured surface. Be careful not to tear it â€“ you want a nice, even layer.
  5. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Brush the dough lightly with olive oil, then spread the pizza sauce evenly, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  7. Sprinkle half of your Mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  8. Add your sliced bell peppers, onion, mushrooms, olives, and spinach uniformly across the pizza.
  9. Top with the remaining Mozzarella, and seasoning with the oregano, crushed red pepper, garlic powder, and a little bit of salt and pepper.
  10. Bake your pizza in the preheated oven for about 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and starts to brown slightly.
